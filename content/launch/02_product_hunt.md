# Product Hunt draft

## Pre-launch checklist
1. Create a Product Hunt account at https://www.producthunt.com (if not yet)
2. Pick a launch date — Tuesday or Wednesday lands best (Mon = stale, Fri = bad). Aim 2 weeks out.
3. Schedule your launch for **00:01 PT** of launch day (so the full 24h window of upvotes is captured).
4. Pre-launch teaser page can be used to build a small subscriber list, but skip if it slows you down.

## Submission fields

### Name
```
Scamira
```

### Tagline (max 60 chars)
```

```
Paste any social-media caption and get a 0-100 score breaking down the funnel structure: Manychat automation, exaggerated income claims, FOMO framing, mechanism opacity, and more. Bilingual (EN/JA). MIT-licensed. Detection prompts 100% public.

## Day-of playbook
- 00:01 PT: Launch goes live, post the first comment
- Then through the day, reply to every comment. Engagement = ranking.
- Share your PH link on X/note/anywhere — but **do not** ask for upvotes (PH bans this). "Check out my launch on Product Hunt" is fine.
- Don't refresh ranking obsessively; check at 6h, 12h, 24h.

## What to expect
- Top 5 of the day: 500-2000 visits to scamira.com
- Top 1 of the day: 5000+ visits
- Not top 10: still ~50-200 visits, decent for SEO backlinks
